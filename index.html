ABOUT ARPS

Assam Rifles Public School, Jorhat was established in October 1977 at its present location at Jorhat. At its initiation it offered I to III classes with a handful of students. In the year 2005 it received affiliation for running XI & XII classes & in the session 2007-08 made history as its first batch of XII went for the boards.
